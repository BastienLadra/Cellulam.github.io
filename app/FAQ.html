  • Question

  • How does Cellulam work?

    Press the end of the lancing device against the side of the fingertip and activate it. You will see a drop of blood form where you have just bitten. Apply the drop of blood to the cassette according to the instructions and wait for the device to display the result of your tests.

  • How do I update Cellulam?

    To update Cellulam, you need the USB cable supplied with your device, the device and a computer. Connect your device to the computer and choose from the options in the UPDATE menu. Go to the Cellulam website in the appropriate tab to do this.

  • How do I transfer my data?

    To transfer your data, you must have the USB cable supplied with your device, the device and a computer. Connect your device to the computer and choose from the options in the TRANSFER menu, then choose the format that suits you best. When connecting click on transfer.

  • Configuration

  • How can I reset Cellulam?

    Press the MENU button then PARAMETER then RESET. This process will reset the device to its default values ​​(factory value).

  • How do I modify my configuration settings?

    Press the MENU button then PARAMETER then CONFIGURATION. A panel of choices is offered to you. Set the one parameter you want and click on SAVE

  • How can I change brightness?

    Press the MENU button then PARAMETER then CONFIGURATION. A panel of choices is offered to you. Adjust the brightness. Five levels are available.

  • How to switch to silent mode

    Keep pressing the power button for 5s. There will be three choices: SLEEP, RESTART, SILENT MODE. Choose silent mode.

Limits for foreign currency transactions online or with PayPal. The limits concerning the countries of receipt of your payments for online transactions or with PayPal. If you know your card is valid but your payment has failed: Contact your card issuer directly. Ask why your card was rejected and if you can still use it. If so, try to renew your transaction.   • Can I pay in multiple times?

    If the amount of your purchase is between 150 and 2000 * euros, we offer a payment solution in 3 or 4 times by credit card. You pay the first installment at the time of purchase, then the next installments are automatically debited at 30 days. The cost of financing is 1.5% of the total amount of your order for payment in 3x and 2.2% of the total amount of your order for payment in 4x. This amount is included in your second installment.

  • Can I specify a user?

  • My data are missing! How do I get them back?

    Each time a test is made, a backup is created in the device memory. Press the RECOVER button to find your missing data.

  • How can I access my Configuration data?

    This action requires the role of a technician because the handling is complex and can cause damage to the internal memory of the device.

  • How can I change my tape configuration?

    There is a valve on the back of the device holding the cassette. Unclip it and change it. If the tape is not played try another one. If it still does not work call a technician.
